About this Course

This course is designed for aspiring ministry leaders and those looking to deepen their spiritual impact within their communities. Over twelve transformative weeks, we will explore the intersection of ancient wisdom and contemporary leadership challenges.

Participants will engage with scriptural foundations, historical case studies of faith-based movements, and practical tools for emotional intelligence and conflict resolution within a spiritual context.
