Mere Christianity

"God whispers to us in our pleasures, speaks in our conscience, but shouts in our pains: it is his megaphone to rouse a deaf world."

Theological Reflection

This passage always hits differently during seasons of trial. Lewis suggests that pain isn't just a consequence of a fallen world, but a tool used by God for redirection. How are we hearing the "megaphone" in our current challenges? I feel like I've been deaf to the whispers lately.

Romans 8:28

"And we know that in all things God works for the good of those who love him, who have been called according to his purpose."

Personal Note

Working through the Greek here, 'synergei' (works together) is such a powerful verb. It implies an active, ongoing weaving of events. Even the messy threads are being woven into the final tapestry. Grateful for this reminder today.
